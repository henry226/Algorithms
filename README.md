# Algorithms
Inserton Sort:
```java
Before insertion sort:
12 9 13 5 6 
Sorting...
array[0]: 9 <=> array[1]: 12
array[2]: 5 <=> array[3]: 13
array[1]: 5 <=> array[2]: 12
array[0]: 5 <=> array[1]: 9
array[3]: 6 <=> array[4]: 13
array[2]: 6 <=> array[3]: 12
array[1]: 6 <=> array[2]: 9
After insertion sort:
5 6 9 12 13 
```
Time Complexity: O(n^2)
Auxiliary Space: O(1)
